During the summer between high school and college (June 2012 - August 2012), I had my first development job, working for Actifio, Inc. Having just taken AP Computer Science my senior year, I familiarized myself with the Android SDK and put my java skills to work. The app, using Actifio's REST API behind a sleek, clean Android UI, allowed Actifio clients to monitor the status of their copy data.
