SarahSullivan.com

Overview

SarahSullivan.com is the personal website for my girlfriend/best friend/partner in crime Sarah Sullivan. Sarah is a Sophomore in Apparel Design at RISD (Rhode Island School of Design), and I am maintaining and developing her site along with my own. Since both sites are very similar, I will refer you to the page on my site for most of the project details, and post here only the information that is specific to this particular project.

Tools

  • HTML
  • Sass
  • Compass
  • Susy
  • Ruby
  • Nanoc

Since it is a reltively small and simple website, I have chosen to use the ruby-based static site generator nanoc, written and actively developed by Denis Defreyne. On the design side of things, I used Compass and Susy because I wanted the benefits of their CSS helpers and grids, but opted to do the design myself with Sass instad of using scaffolding form frameworks like Blueprint or Bootstrap.
