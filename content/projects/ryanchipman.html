Though I registered this domain name and put up some placeholder pages back in winter 2012-2013, it was not until January 2014 that I started putting serious work into developing this site. This site is my online portfolio for showcasing me and my work, but has also served as a great opportunity for me to flex my web development and design muscles.

<% end %> <% content_for :tools do %>
  • HTML
  • Sass
  • Compass
  • Susy
  • Ruby
  • Nanoc

Since it is a reltively small and simple website, I have chosen to use the ruby-based static site generator nanoc, written and actively developed by Denis Defreyne. On the design side of things, I used Compass and Susy because I wanted the benefits of their CSS helpers and grids, but opted to do the design myself with Sass instad of using scaffolding form frameworks like Blueprint or Bootstrap.
